Satnam Industries - Where quality meets precision.

Satnam Industries, established in 1992 and based in Faridabad, is a premier sheet metal manufacturing and fabrication company. With a skilled workforce of 85 employees, we provide comprehensive solutions tailored to meet the demands of diverse industries. Our state-of-the-art facilities boast advanced machinery, including a 6 kW laser cutting machine, CNC turret punching, hydraulic press machines, and specialized welding technologies.

We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality products such as VRLA and Li-Ion battery cabinets, enclosures for heavy electrical equipment, air force cabins, and chassis for E-rickshaws. Serving a wide range of sectors, including smart city projects, energy storage, and defense, we consistently meet international standards of excellence.

Satnam Industries has a spacious 21,600 sq. ft. facility, equipped with modern tools like 3D AutoCAD, SolidWorks, and AP100 for in-house design and development. Our achievements include ISO 9001:2015 and ISO 45001 certifications, reinforcing our commitment to quality, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Guided by our vision of fairness, innovation, and global reach, we strive to build lasting relationships with customers, partners, and employees. Join us as we continue to pioneer versatile and sustainable manufacturing solutions tailored to your needs.

About Us

Our Products

At Satnam Industries, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality sheet metal products tailored to diverse industry needs. Our product portfolio includes precision-engineered metal enclosures, VRLA and Li-Ion battery cabinets, and chassis for E-Rickshaws. We also provide custom solutions for electrical panels, defense systems, and smart city projects, including air force cabins and heavy electrical equipment enclosures.

Utilizing advanced machinery like laser cutting machines, CNC turret punching machines, and hydraulic presses, we ensure each product meets the highest quality standards. Our fabrication capabilities include MIG, TIG, and spot welding for aluminum, stainless steel, and mild steel materials.

With a commitment to innovation and durability, our products cater to industries such as energy storage, automotive, defense, and general manufacturing. Every product is tested for precision, ensuring reliability and customer satisfaction.
